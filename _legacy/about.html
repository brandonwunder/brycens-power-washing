About AZ Concrete King

Arizona's trusted name in concrete power washing.

Our Story

AZ Concrete King was founded with a simple mission: to help Arizona homeowners restore the beauty of their concrete surfaces. We know how the Arizona sun, dust, and weather can take a toll on driveways, patios, and walkways.

What started as a passion for making things look new again has grown into a trusted local business serving residential customers across the Phoenix metro area. We take pride in every job, whether it's a small walkway or a full-property cleaning.

Our team uses professional-grade equipment and proven techniques to deliver results that speak for themselves. We believe in honest pricing, reliable service, and making sure every customer is satisfied.
